Scope:

The workshop will cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

  • Deep Learning architectures and applications.
  • Machine Learning algorithms and techniques.
  • Big Data analytics and management.
  • Al technologies and their real-world applications.

The scope will also extend to practical implementation, allowing participants to work on relevant projects and case studies, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

Objectives:

  • Provide a thorough understanding of the latest advancements in Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Big Data, and AI.
  • Foster hands-on experience through practical exercises and project work.
  • Facilitate networking and knowledge-sharing among participants.
  • Encourage collaborative problem-solving and critical thinking in the context of real-world scenarios.
  • Empower participants to apply acquired knowledge to their professional roles.
